Rose Rocketry

Rose Rocketry provides Rose-Hulman students a platform to explore and engage with aerospace fields. Through rocketry competitions, nanosatellite development, liquid propulsion systems, and experimental rocketry, we aim to develop students' skills and prepare them for careers in both industry and research.

Our Subteams

USLI

Annual NASA competition to design and launch a high-powered rocket with a scientific payload.

RPL

A four-year challenge focused on developing a lander vehicle powered by a custom liquid rocket engine.

HPR

Hobby rocketry with a focus on building Level 1 and Level 2 certified rockets.
